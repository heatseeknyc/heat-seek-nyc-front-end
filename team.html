CORE TEAM

TRISTAN SIEGAL

Co-Founder

From the project's beginning Tristan's been in charge of user experience as well as sharing back-end developing duties with Heat Seek's other co-founder, William.

WILLIAM

C0-Founder

William is a web developer with a passion for social justice. He's a rubyist, a yogi, and a super friendly person. He has been on the project since the beginning and is always ready to answer technical questions from any area of the project, or just about why its such important work.

ANDREA ACEVEDO

Design Lead

Andrea comes from the public service design world working previously at the City of Boston branding SMEs and initiatives and more recently at Participle where she designed new services that tackled social issues in the UK. She was fortunate to stumble upon Heat Seekâ€™s co-founders at the Big Apps launch and will be working on the design of the app and helping out on the UX.

DANIEL KRONOVET

Hareware Lead

A classmate of Tristan and William's at the Flatiron School, Daniel joined up as the Hardware Lead shortly after the founders decided to take the project to the BigApps. Currently employed as a software engineer with ParagonMeasure, a health technology startup, Daniel is excited about helping improve the sensor system which drives Heat Seek.

JARRYD HAMMEL

Business Development Lead

Jarryd comes to Heat Seek NYC with several years of experience as a corporate finance advisor. He is currently pursuing his MBA at the Wharton School. As Biz Dev Lead, Jarryd is focused on refining Heat Seek NYC's message and building the community and organizational ties we need to maximize our social impact.

TOM

Marketing Lead

Tom has an eclectic background ranging from hedge fund analyst to parade mascot. He joins Heat Seek NYC to help further our mission of keeping the heat on in NYC and to help pioneer the algorithmic advocacy movement.

ETHAN OZELIUS

Front-end Lead

As Lead Front-end developer, Ethan works with a great team of designers and developers, to build an efficient website for all users.
